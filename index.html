Murati Suites Sarandë

Welcome to Murati Suites Sarandë, your ultimate beachfront escape in the heart of Sarandë. Located mere steps from La Petite Beach and a short walk from Maestral Beach, our 3-star hotel perfectly combines comfort and convenience. Revel in breathtaking sea views from our terrace and unwind in our air-conditioned rooms, each featuring free WiFi, a private bathroom, a wardrobe, and a flat-screen TV. Some rooms also offer a private balcony for an elevated experience.
